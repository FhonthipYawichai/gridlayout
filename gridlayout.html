Morning Half Day Tour

Price 999 baht/person(from 1900 baht)

Overview

Get up close and personal with the world’s largest land mammal on a visit to the family-owned Elephant Sanctuary.
The elephants here once worked on logging crews to cut down the protected forest, but today they live at leisure.
This small-group adventure, limited to just nine travelers, includes a Thai lunch, water, skip-the-line access to the
sanctuary, and round-trip transfers from Chiang Mai hotels. Learn how to care for elephants at the Elephant Sanctuary
Meet the family who own the sanctuary and care for the animals Comprehensive package with Thai lunch and bottled water
No motorbike or taxi stress: Includes 2-way transfers from Chiang Mai hotels
